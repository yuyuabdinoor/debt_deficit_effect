### 4Ô∏è‚É£ **Contagion Mechanics**

#### How Crises Spread:

**Wave 1 (Immediate Impact - Days to Weeks)**
- Direct trading partners affected first
- Impact: 70-90% of epicenter's crisis
- Mechanism: Trade disruption, currency effects

**Wave 2 (Secondary Impact - Weeks to Months)**
- Partners of partners affected
- Impact: 50-70% of original crisis
- Mechanism: Supply chain disruption, confidence loss

**Wave 3 (Tertiary Impact - Months)**
- Broader regional effects
- Impact: 30-50% of original crisis
- Mechanism: Regional economic slowdown

**Example: Nigeria Default Scenario**
- **Wave 1**: Ghana (85%), Angola (78%), Togo (72%)
- **Wave 2**: Ivory Coast (65%), Senegal (58%)
- **Wave 3**: Regional economic slowdown
- **Total Affected**: 8 countries (57% of network)
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months for full cascade
